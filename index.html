Welcome to Nulito

We Are Edible Oil Company

Nulito started its illustrious journey in 2014 with the setting up of its founding production unit in Surat.The hard work, grit, and determination coupled with the best business practises led Nulito to surpass many glorious milestones.

People’s trust and confidence in the brand was the biggest factor behind setting up of various production units situated at Ahmedabad, Rajkot, Mumbai,and Kadi.

Groundnut Oil is well-known for its long shelf life and purity. We have been superior to the market in all aspects in market share owing to the satisfaction of our esteemed consumers. Having pioneered in the sector of Groundnut Oil, Nulito created its own bouquet of high quality edible oils and products (e.g. Peanut Butter) that have seen an unprecedented acceptance in the market not just in Gujarat but across the country.

The entire range of Nulito stand testimony to company’a dedication to both customers and society.
